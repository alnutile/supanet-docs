SupaNet comes in two flavors. This page is for the **hosted version** — you sign up, pay, and we handle the infrastructure. If you want to run it yourself, see [Deploy](/docs/building/deploy).

## Sign up for a hosted workspace

Visit **supanet.io** and click "Start your workspace" to begin.

1. **Enter your details:** work email, password, and your workspace name (e.g., "Acme Co").
2. **Confirm payment:** you'll go straight to Stripe Checkout. SupaNet is **$49 per month**, flat rate — no per-seat fees, no hidden costs. Cancel anytime.
3. **Provisioning:** after payment confirms, we automatically set up your workspace. A progress page shows you what's happening (database creation, security setup, AI services starting). This usually takes about a minute.
4. **Ready to go:** once it's done, you'll get an email with a link to your workspace (e.g., `https://acme.supanet.io`). Sign in with the email and password you created.

## What's included

Your $49/month workspace includes:

- A **dedicated Supabase database** — your data lives on infrastructure you can trust.
- **Unlimited team members** — no per-seat pricing, no seat limits.
- Your own **AI allowance** — about $10/month worth of OpenRouter credits, included.
- **All SupaNet features** — chat, artifacts, automations, webhooks, Slack integration, everything.
- Ability to **pause or cancel anytime** — your workspace pauses at the end of your billing period, and you have 30 days to export your data if you want to leave.

## After you sign in

Your first sign-in makes you an admin. You land in the home dashboard, which gives you a quick tour of what's possible. From there:

- **Chat** with your team's AI assistant about your business.
- **Upload files and PDFs** and ask questions about them.
- **Create automations** (scheduled tasks, webhooks, Slack bots) without writing code.
- **Invite team members** in Settings → Invite people.
- **Connect external tools** (Slack, custom APIs, external MCP servers) if you need them.

Everything syncs in real time, so your whole team sees updates instantly.

## Managing your subscription

- **Billing & invoices:** visit the Stripe customer portal (linked in your email or in workspace Settings).
- **Pause or cancel:** one click in the Stripe portal. Your workspace pauses at the period end, and you can pick it up again by restarting your subscription.
- **Add team members:** Settings → Invite people. You can invite by email or share an invite link.
- **AI usage over your allowance:** we meter it and bill it automatically on your next invoice, with margin built in.

## Export or eject

If you ever want to move on, you have two paths:

1. **Export your data:** at any time, download all your artifacts, files, and data.
2. **Eject to self-hosted:** we can transfer your Supabase project to your own account and hand you the open-source repo. Your workspace keeps working, and you're now running your own copy — no vendor lock-in.

## Troubleshooting

**Your workspace isn't showing up after provisioning finishes?**

If the "Your workspace is ready" email is delayed or the link doesn't work, try:
1. Refreshing your browser.
2. Checking that you're signed in to the right email.
3. Visiting your workspace URL directly: `https://yourworkspacename.supanet.io`.
