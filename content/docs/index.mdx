SupaNet is a private workspace with an AI assistant that actually knows your
stuff. You chat with it, share living documents, drop in PDFs and ask questions
about them, and wire it into the rest of your tools. It runs on
[Supabase](https://supabase.com), and the whole thing is yours to run, host,
and extend.

## The short version

You sign in to a workspace. There is a chat assistant in the middle of it. That
assistant can read the files and PDFs your team uploads, run little tools, save
documents you can share with a link, and react to events coming in from other
systems. Admins decide what tools and prompts are switched on. Everything is
protected per-user by the database itself, so people only see what they are
allowed to see.

If you just want to *use* it, head to [Using SupaNet](/docs/using). If you want
to *run or extend* it, head to [Building on SupaNet](/docs/building).

## A small meta note

SupaNet can read its own documentation. These pages can be pushed into SupaNet's
knowledge base so the in-app assistant can answer "how do I do X?" using the
exact same words you are reading now. That loop is described in
[Teach SupaNet about itself](/docs/building/teach-supanet-about-itself) - humans
read the docs here, and the assistant reads them too.
